This is the social platform you were waiting for.
Main features are:

  • You can create and manage your own virtual events and affairs.
  • The information will be held private and will be deleted once expired.
  • An invitation is required to join the events. You can refuse it or accept it, pincler will keep the count.
  • Registered users may join an event with one click. Otherwise you need to create an account.
  • The most important, an event has a deadline: it will expire once finished !

Why is pincler interesting to me?
You can join or create events without having to share all your wall:

  • The coach can schedule the next game easily with all the team.
    ... You will know all the details with time in advance.
  • You can share a birthday party with all the parents in the class.
    ... You will know easily how many children will assist !
  • Are you going to be married soon ??
    ... Put aside the boring email lists, use pincler instead.
    ... You may know how many friends will attend so there won't be any surprise !

FAQ

Is this a proof of concept?
This is a project from Coursera's "Startup Engineering" course.
The project will evolve from conceptual design, development and cloud deployment in further stages.
What is the scope of the project ?
The idea is to make it functional in a first milestone. There are enough challenges with this primary objective ! In a second stage there will be integrations with third party networks and apps.
